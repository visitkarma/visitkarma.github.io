## About Visit Karma

We're a city-dwelling family with a love for luxury travel and outdoor adventures — and **Visit Karma** is where we share what we actually experience on the road.

From ski-in/ski-out hotels in the Alps to overwater villas in the Maldives and all-inclusive resorts in the Caribbean, every review here is based on a stay we took ourselves. We write the honest, detailed guides we wish we'd had before booking: what the rooms are really like, whether the dining lives up to the price, how family-friendly a property is, and whether it's worth the splurge.

## What you'll find here

- **First-hand hotel and resort reviews** — Marriott, all-inclusives, design hotels, and more
- **Family travel planning** — getting the most out of trips without overspending
- **Destination notes** from the Caribbean, the Alps, the Maldives, and beyond
